About Elijah

Elijah Mullenax is an aspiring writer and musician in the Chapel Hill/Carrboro area. Originally from Brevard, North Carolina, he has been following these creative pursuits from a young age. He is in the process of writing his first novel, “Jimmy’s Painted World” and frequently busks in the local area. He is in his second year at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. He has always loved music and reading and he hoped one day he would have the opportunity to create his own content. He believes in “practical minimalism” both in life and otherwise.
